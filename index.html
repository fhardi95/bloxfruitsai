Blox Fruits Trade Calculator – Everything You Need to Know

What Is This Calculator?

The BloxCalc.gg trade calculator is a free, community-maintained tool that helps Blox Fruits players evaluate trades before confirming them. Simply add the items you are offering and the items the other player is offering, and the calculator instantly totals up the value, average demand score, and approximate Robux price of both sides.

Whether you are a beginner picking up your first Permanent Fruit or a veteran chasing the rarest mythical items, this tool removes guesswork and prevents you from being cheated in a trade.

How Are Values Determined?

Values are crowd-sourced from thousands of active Blox Fruits traders and refined by our editorial team. We weigh recent trade logs, demand polls in our Discord, and in-game supply changes caused by events, updates, and fruit removals. Values are updated at minimum twice per week.

Each fruit also carries a Demand score (0–10) that reflects how many buyers are actively searching for it. A fruit with a high value but low demand may be harder to trade away, so always factor both numbers into your decision.

Reading the Verdict

Fair Trade – Both sides are within 10% of each other in total value. This is the safest outcome.

Overpay – You are giving more value than you receive. You may still accept if demand is on your side or you need the item urgently.

Underpay – You are receiving more value than you give. The other trader may walk away. Consider offering an extra low-value item to make it feel balanced.

Tips for Better Blox Fruits Trades

Always compare demand, not just value. A fruit valued at 500K but with 3/10 demand is harder to move than one at 400K with 9/10 demand. Check the trend arrow — a rising fruit today may be worth even more tomorrow after a balance update.

Use the calculator for every trade, no matter how simple it seems. Experienced scammers rely on you skipping this step for "obvious" deals. A 10-second check here can save hours of grinding.
